PRIVATE LIFE

My Story

During my apprenticeship as an accountant, I had the chance to work on a website for the first time. That experience changed everything - I realized I wanted to pursue a career in this direction. I transitioned into marketing, but it still wasn’t quite the right fit. It wasn’t until I discovered UX during my studies in Web and Usability Engineering that everything clicked. I knew this was what I truly wanted to do - which led me to where I am today. 😀
