HI - I'M ANTHONY

'I'm not only interested in whether things are beautiful, but also whether they are functional'

I'm a UX/UI Designer from Zurich, Switzerland. I enjoy taking complex problems and turning them into simple and beautiful interface designs. My background in Web & Usability Engineering and my current Master’s in UX Design at FHGR help me balance empathy with structure, research with creativity.

I focus on removing friction. That means diving into research early, building lean prototypes fast, and paying close attention to tone and clarity in every UI element. Whether I’m mapping complex flows or testing microcopy with users, I keep things focused on real needs, not just features.
