ABOUT ME

Hi - I'm Anthony!

I'm a Master's part-time student at FHGR based in Zurich, building on my Bachelor's thesis about boosting UX maturity inside organizations. My expertise lies in prototyping, UI design, and user research, using tools like Figma and Adobe Creative Cloud to bring ideas to life. Over the years, I have worked on diverse projects ranging from platforms that support young people to optimizing e-commerce for better usability.

When I’m not designing, you’ll likely find me sketching in my notebook, skiing down Swiss slopes, traveling to discover new perspectives, or immersed in a good book or video game. I enjoy moments that balance focus and play, and I often find that creativity outside the screen sparks better ideas on it.
